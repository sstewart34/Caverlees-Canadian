About the Job

Job Description

Position Title: Senior Desktop and Infrastructure Engineer

Location: Tampa, FL

Reports To: President

Type of Position: Full-Time

 

Position Overview

The Senior Desktop and Infrastructure Engineer will be responsible for ensuring optimal performance and ongoing support, of the firms desktop, server, network, and telecommunications infrastructure as the firm grows in size and complexity.

Responsibilities

         Responsible for all desktop / laptop / tablet technology for the Tampa office including setup, configuration, and troubleshooting / maintenance

         Responsible for IT operations and maintenance of LAN, voice, video-conference/ Cisco Telepresence, and overall IT infrastructure in the Tampa office

         Work with IT staff (both internal and external) to help implement a broad set of Hedge Fund business applications

         Pro-actively work with IT and Business management to identify and implement new technology

         Ensure WAN infrastructure between Tampa and Boston offices remains reliable, redundant, and  operates at optimal speed and capacity through maintenance and proactive planning

         Implement robust change management procedures for desktop and server application installations that minimize down-time (image management, .msi packaging, etc.)

         User account provisioning including Exchange Server

         Responsible for handling Patch Management, Antivirus Management, and Asset Management of all desktop equipment and software

         Provision and maintain iPhone, Blackberry, and other mobile devices

         Break/fix desktop support

         File and print management

         Conduct quarterly disaster recovery/BCP test and document results

         Act as primary escalation point in the Tampa office for all technology support issues

Qualifications

         Bachelors or Masters degree in Engineering, Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related discipline

         4+ years of technical work experience

         Deep understanding of Financial Services technology such as Bloomberg Terminal, Market Data, etc...

         Experience with Active Directory Administration, MS Exchange Server and, Cisco networking equipment

         Experience with virtual server, workload management and overall IT monitoring

         Experience supporting multi-site networks

         Experience with Windows XP and Windows 7 desktop support

         Experience with MS Office Suite

         Experience with Citrix XenDesktop VDI solutions a plus

         Experience with Cisco VOIP & Telepresence technology a plus

         Experience with ITIL and other IT management best practices a plus

Personal Attributes

         Must have outstanding problem solving capabilities with strong analytical skills

         Able to handle multiple tasks concurrently

         Ability to work independently, identify priorities, and be flexible

         Learn quickly, and work in a fast-paced environment

         Excellent written and verbal communication skills

         Excellent technical skills

         Ability to translate technical issues and impact analysis to the business for appropriate prioritization etc...

         Ability to work at both the strategic and tactical level

         Strong attention to detail and follow-through
