About the Job

Insight is a leading IT solution provider of IT products and services. Insight offers over 200,000 brand-name IT products from leading manufacturers, such as HP, IBM, Intel, Cisco, Microsoft and more. Insight's comprehensive services offering assists customers with implementation and integration of the latest IT solutions organization-wide. Small and Medium Business (SMB), Enterprise customers and government and education customers can turn to one partner for hardware, software, peripheral, service and solution needs. Insight is an Equal Opportunity Employer M/F/D/V.

Insight Enterprises, Inc is currently hiring a Consulting Architect in Boston. The Practice Architect (PA) is responsible for supporting pre-sales, creating repeatable solutions and methodologies and for providing project quality assurance and escalation support. The Practice Architects work closely with Market Technical Leaders on improving repeatable solutions and delivery methodologies. The Practice Architect is a billable resource, primarily serving as technical oversight and quality assurance on projects and has a utilization target of 30%. 40% of the Practice Architect time is spent supporting the Practice Leader and sales teams in pre-sales effort. The remaining 30% of the Practice Architects time is spent creating and improving solutions and methodologies.


WORK PERFORMED


To be successful in this role, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.


Essential Functions of the Job:


  • Defines and enforces practice-specific delivery methodology, templates and tools; the practice-specific methodology will always fit within Insights overall service delivery methodology


  • Develops repeatable packaged offerings


  • Provides technical review and approval for all services estimates, proposals, statement of works, and RFPs.


  • Provides oversight and QA for each project within the practice


  • Assists with technical escalation from Market Technical Leaders


  • Reviews and approves professional services estimates, statements of work and change orders


  • Assists Market Technical Leaders in technical vetting of full time employees and contractors


  • Assists Practice Leader in build out of technical development plans


  • Supports the practice lead (business lead) in business development and sales engineering; acts as the Subject Matter Expert for the practice


  • Coordinates with Practice Architects from other practices to continually improve our delivery methodology and share best practices and lessons learned


MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS


Education and/or Experience:


A bachelors degree in a technical discipline or demonstrated performance in a technical work environment is required. A current advanced certification in the practice area assigned is required for this position. Minimum eight years of experience architecting, designing and implementing advanced security and network solutions. Minimum four years of experience managing technical staff in a fast-paced environment. Previous consulting/professional services experience is required.


KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITIES


Strong ability to cultivate relationships with partners, suppliers in order to develop and share understanding of technology roadmaps both within Insight and with Insights client community required. Excellent written and verbal communication skills with the ability to articulate both technical and abstract concepts in a clear and concise manner a must. Ability to build relationships and communicate effectively required. Must be able to multi-task effectively. Ability to interface effectively with client executives in a professional and businesslike manner is a must. Must have prior supervisory experience in areas of, mentoring, training, staff development, etc.


Demonstrated experience in architecting and implementing complex compute, networking, and virtualization solutions.


Advanced knowledge of but not limited to:


  • VMware vSphere


  • VMware vCenter Site Recovery Manager


  • VMware View


  • VMware vCloud Director


  • Cisco Unified Computing System


  • HP BladeSystem with VirtualConnect/Flex10


  • Converged Infrastructure solutions like Vblock, FlexPod, and HP CloudMatrix


  • Data Center Operations

Working knowledge of Citrix and Microsoft based virtualization technologies is a plus but not a requirement.


Working knowledge of Fabric and Storage infrastructure technologies is a plus but not a requirement.


Minimum preferred certifications:


  • VMware Certified Professional (VCP)


  • Cisco Data Center Unified Computing Design and /or Support Specialist DCUCD and/or DCUCI


  • Cisco Date Center Networking Infrastructure Design and/or Support Specialist DCNID and/or DCNS


  • HP Accredited System Engineer -- HP BladeSystem

Desired certifications:


  • VMware Certified Advanced Professional Data Center Design


  • VMware Certified Advanced Professional Data Center Administration


  • VMware Certified Design Expert (VCDX)

Additional certifications in Severs (HP, IBM, Cisco), Storage (EMC, NetApp, HP) and Network (Cisco, HP) a plus
