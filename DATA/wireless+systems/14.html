About the Job

JOB TITLE: TECHNICAL SERVICE DELIVERY ARCHITECT (NETWORK)


Location: Portland, OR


Terms: Full Time, Direct Hire


Travel: Up to 25%




Summary:


Responsible for leading the efficient delivery of IT Infrastructure services to the business units and customers. Manage day-to-day relationship with Service Provider to ensure effective delivery of outsourced/managed services. Work closely with the Service Provider on the execution of transformational initiatives to achieve IT Infrastructure strategic vision for Con-way.



Manage highly diverse technical staff that is responsible for providing IT infrastructure design, engineering, disaster recovery and project management services. The manager will act as chief liaison between IT application teams and the outsourced infrastructure service provider. Participate in the definition of a cohesive Infrastructure strategy with the Enterprise Architecture Team and the IT Leadership team.



Essential Functions:



  • Collaborate with Infrastructure Service Provider on the implementation of solutions for all infrastructure services
  • Collaborate with Enterprise Architecture on Infrastructure roadmaps, standards, processes and guidelines. Ensure integrity of infrastructure components in alignment with stated architectures
  • Act as technology liaison between Application Teams and Service Provider
  • Research new technologies in response to project requirements and business needs in lab environment
  • Recognize constraints of specific infrastructure architectures and provide alternate scalable solutions in order to meet business and project requirements
  • Recommend performance improvements to the Service Provider and Application Development teams from an application perspective
  • Assist service provider with technology troubleshooting from an application perspective
  • Assist in Service Provider governance by collaborating on service level guidelines, key performance metrics and critical metrics
  • Mentor application development team members to ensure functionality is aligned with established technology methodologies and practices
  • Mentor other infrastructure team members on service levels and performance metrics




Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:



  • Bachelors degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related discipline generally required. Will accept any combination of equivalent education, training and experience.
  • 10+ years of in-depth experience in design, engineering and support of end user computing and collaboration services. Including 2+ years experience in mentoring and leading other team members.
  • End user devices are defined as; laptops, desktops, handheld/rugged devices, RF guns, Printers, Thin Client Devices, PDA, Smart phones, Cell phones, device peripherals that connect to our network
  • Understanding of wireless technologies (WLAN / WWAN) such as GSM, CDMA, iDEN, 802.11 and their evolutionary paths
  • Assess wide array of end user technologies and recognize their impact on current state vs. future state architecture. As well, how these technologies will integrate with current infrastructure
  • Understanding of virtualization technologies such as VMware, virtual desktop, Citrix and Citrix XenApp
  • Solid knowledge of mobile operating systems including application packaging, delivery, security, lockdown and general systems management
  • Solid understanding of GPS, RFID, 1D/2D scanning with the ability to recognize deployment opportunities
  • Understand Collaboration technologies like Sharepoint, IM, Video conferencing, etc.
  • Ability to rapidly learn new technologies and recommend those determined to be beneficial to the company
  • Experience in development of technology requirements for execution by service provider is desired
  • Experience in development and implementation of technology roadmaps, standards and processes is desired
  • Relevant technology certifications (Operating System certification and/or vendor technology certification) are a plus
  • ITIL knowledge, certification and work experience in a process oriented environment is desired
  • Prior experience working in an outsource service provider environment is a plus
  • Demonstrated analytical capabilities, ability to effectively identify and resolve technology issues in diverse technology environments
  • Demonstrated ability to decompose large complex systems into logical subsystems with well defined boundaries and integration points.
  • Strong interpersonal and leadership skills, ability to work effectively in a dynamic environment with a broad range of end-user configurations
  • Strong communication skills (oral and written). Communicate effectively with end users, management, vendors and partners
  • Ability to visualize ideas into diagrams, presentations and requirement documents
  • Occasional travel to business unit and partner locations may be required
  • Demonstrated ability to maintain confidential and sensitive information
  • Provide leadership within the team and mentor teammates to increase overall team capabilities. Partner with manager and other business unit leaders to facilitate dynamic and effective group management
About Con-way


Con-way Enterprise Services, located in northwest Portland, includes the companys Information Technology, Finance and Administration teams. The Enterprise Services campus consists of approximately 700 professional services employees, and borders The Pearl, near Legacy Good Samaritan Hospital in Northwest Portland. Our campus includes an employee gym with locker rooms and showers, two cafeterias, secured bicycle barn, and free parking. Our Total Rewards package includes medical, dental and vision coverage, life insurance, disability, 401(k), stock purchase plan, and TriMet subsidy.


Con-way Inc. (NYSE:CNW), our parent company, is a $5.3 billion freight transportation and logistics services company headquartered in Ann Arbor, MI. An industry leader combining the resources of over 28,500 professionals in the transportation and logistics industry, Con-way delivers industry-leading services through its primary operating companies of Con-way Freight, Con-way Truckload, and Menlo Worldwide Logistics. These operating units provide high-performance, day-definite less-than-truckload (LTL), full truckload and intermodal freight transportation; logistics, warehousing and supply chain management services; and trailer manufacturing. Con-way and its subsidiaries operate from more than 440 operating locations across North America and in 18 countries across five continents.
