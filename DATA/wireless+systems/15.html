About the Job

 Raytheon is seeking a Wireless Engineer to fill the following Job Description:

Raytheon is currently in search of a candidate with a strong technical and operational background to be part of our Wireless Engineering team.  Wireless Engineers provides continuity between Operations personnel (both in CONUS and OCONUS locations) and the engineering and development teams.  The Wireless Engineer's primary role is to be the voice of operations, advocating on their behalf to provide feedback and guidance to the engineering teams.  They are the "honest broker" who is an unbiased voice that can draw on both their technical expertise in wireless and radio frequency (RF) communications and operational experience to effectively evaluate systems, provide input to the Contracting Officer Technical Representative (COTR), and provide continuity throughout the development cycle.  Wireless Engineers are an integral part of the customer organization, ensuring that operations personnel have the tools to successfully, safely and efficiently complete their operational objectives.  The Customer organization reaches across multiple Intelligence Community (IC) Agencies and internal offices; Wireless Engineers must be dynamic, with outstanding interpersonal skills and the ability to work in diverse teams easily building a rapport with those around them.

Wireless Engineers are expected to keep on the forefront of wireless/RF technology.  The exponential rate of technological developments in this area creates an ever challenging array of possible changes.  Innovative ideas are encouraged by all members of the office.

The importance of the client mission will at times require candidates to work outside their area of expertise and learn new skills.

Security Clearance:  Minimum TS with Poly

Travel:  10 to 15% primarily CONUS but some OCONUS TDYs will be required.

Required Skills:

  • 8+ years of practical experience

  • Demonstrated ability translating operational needs into technical requirements

  • Demonstrated operational/field experience

  • Must be able to work in multi-discipline teams

  • Attention to detail and the ability to operate in a dynamic environment

  • Excellent written and verbal skills

  • Outstanding interpersonal skills

  • Willing to learn new skills and continue to expand current knowledge of Wireless and RF Technologies

  • Understanding of the Intelligence Community (IC)

  • Strong written and oral communications skills.  Ability to author technical documents and provide briefings

  • Proficient understanding of Radio Frequency (RF) propagation theory and the differences in communication over varies frequencies

  • Familiarity with the Program Management Framework (PMF)

  • Demonstrated knowledge of the systems engineering lifecycle process to include control gates, technical documentation and requirements gathering

  • Must be willing to support CONUS and OCONUS temporary duty (TDY) stations as needed by the customer.  TDYs will typically be short in duration, less than 1 week and no more than 2 weeks.

  • Proficient in at least two (2) of the following Wireless Radio Frequency (RF) areas:

    o   At least 8 years relevant experience in telecommunications with 3G Cellular and 4G Cellular data technologies to include LTE and WiMax

    o   EDGE/HSPA/HSPA+

    o   Terrestrial short-to-mid range VHF/UHF Communications (i.e. Push to Talk and tactical communications methods)

    o   High Frequency (HF) Communications

    o   Satellite Communications (SATCOM)

    o   Wifi protocols

    o   Nano/Femto/Pico/Macro cells

    o   Mandatory experience - at least five years experience with cellular technologies including GSM, CDMA, HSPA+, UMTS, and LTE

    Desired Skills:

  • Operational experience and familiarity with customer's tools and equipment for wireless missions

  • Familiarity with signal and communications exploitation

  • Background in SIGINT

  • Knowledge of IC mission functions by Agency

  • Ability to train others

  • Operational experience that has utilized/leveraged customer systems

  • Experience interfacing with high level officials (GS15 and higher)

    Desired Experience:

  • At least 8 years relevant experience in telecommunications
