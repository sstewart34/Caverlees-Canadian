About the Job

The User Experience (UX) Designer will own the look and feel of Autonomous Solutions, Inc.s flagship command and control software package for unmanned ground vehicles, Mobius. The designer will work with the Director of Product Management, the Director of Software Engineering, and software engineer teams to ensure that upgrades and new releases are aesthetically and functionally sound, and are moving toward simplicity for end users. The UX designer will be the linchpin between developers and end users to develop software assistance tools, storyboards, prototypes, and workflows that will increase the comfort, usability, and productivity of all ASI software solutions.


Classification: Exempt
Location: Petersboro, Utah


Essential Education, Work Experience, Job Skills

  • Bachelors or Masters degree in computer science, cognitive psychology, human-computer interaction, or other related field
  • 4-6 years of experience in UX design with a proven track record of successful design implementations
  • Understanding of and experience with Agile/SCRUM software development techniques
  • Demonstrate a high degree of creativity, problem-solving skills, attention to detail, rapid prototyping skills, and passion for usability and simplicity
  • Demonstrate teamwork, negotiation, and presentation skills with the ability to sell ideas
  • Sound understanding of user-centered design principles and tools
  • Experience using graphic design tools

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

  • Develop an expert-level understanding of competing and complimentary software solutions in the industry
  • Collect and define innovative user interface designs
  • Imagine, storyboard, prototype, and implement software interface designs that simplify user experience and increase user productivity
  • Work with steering committee members, product managers, and software developers to ensure that ASI software products reflect customer needs and maintain consistency across features
  • Engage as a team member with Agile/SCRUM software development teams
  • Persuasively convey design solutions to the executive team, product managers, developers, and customers
  • Conduct formative and summative usability tests, contextual inquiries, surveys, and interviews with focus groups and end users
  • Formulate UX design standards that can be used to guide future software development

The above statements describe the general nature and level of work being performed in this job function. They are not intended to be an exhaustive list of all duties, and indeed additional responsibilities may be assigned, as required, by Autonomous Solutions, Inc. The employee must possess the essential knowledge and skills described below.


Company Description


Autonomous Solutions, Inc. designs and manufactures unmanned vehicle systems, software, and components for a variety of industrial and military customers. We have extensive experience in the automation of both large and small scale robotic vehicles with an emphasis on multi-vehicle command and control, mission planning, sensor fusion, obstacle avoidance, tele-operation, and point-and-click ease of use.


ASI was founded in 2000 by a group of researchers in order to take the ideas and technologies developed at Utah State University into the commercial sector. From this beginning, ASI has grown to become one of the largest privately held robotics-focused companies in the world. We employ a team of world class computer, electrical, and mechanical engineers and support staff at our 100 acre proving ground facility in Northern Utah.
