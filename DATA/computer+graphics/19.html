About the Job

Governmental Management Services, Inc. is seeking an Activities Assistant - Communications to join our team at Lake Ashton, an upscale gated residential community in Lake Wales, FL. Full time employees qualify for benefits such as health insurance, paid time off, paid holidays, 401K and other benefits. This is a part time (29 hours weekly) or full time (40 hours weekly) position with a starting compensation of $11.00 hourly.

JOB SUMMARY: The primary purpose of the Lake Ashton activities staff is to provide Lake Ashton residents and guests with activities and programs that enhance an active adult lifestyle. The secondary mission of the staff is to communicate these activities using multiple delivery systems. This communications position is responsible to provide layout and design for print and electronic publications under the direction of the Community Director.

KNOWLEDEGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES:

Ability to manage multiple projects, set priorities, and exercise sound judgment

Demonstrated ability in verbal and written communication skills

Skilled in the use of the Internet, spreadsheets, databases, and word processing software

Ability to process computer data and to format and generate reports

Ability to create, compose, and edit written materials

Thorough knowledge of grammar and strong writing ability

Strong attention to detail

Essential computer skills using Microsoft Office software including Power Point and Publisher

Ability to use layout and design skills to design attractive communication tools such as newsletter, posters, electronic slides and website pages

Ability to quickly foster goodwill while working with resident volunteers, committees and employees

Ability to promote and show enthusiasm for Lake Ashton activities

Knowledge and understanding of business concepts

Ability to apply policies and procedures to solve issues

Performs duties that require initiative and strong communication skills

Ability to respond to common inquiries or complaints

EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE: College-level coursework in Communication, Graphics or a related field is preferred. A successful track record of excellent communication and high quality publications is required.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES:

Provides layout and design services and support for monthly newsletter and other assigned publications, both print and electronic

Take photographs for print and electronic publications

Prepare files for production on-press/printer

Prepare website postings and graphics

Propose and develop themes for Lake Ashton activities and event promotions

Assist in managing advertising sales and meeting revenue goals for newsletter advertising revenue

Assist in maintaining other activities communication media such as local access cable channel and community information meetings

Assist in daily operations of the Activities Office including processing incoming and outgoing mail and forms, managing a multi line phone system and interacting with customers

Assist in communication related to resident services

Assist residents who call or come to the office

Answer prospect and resident inquiries and concerns which may include questions about the surrounding area, the community, the company, amenities, etc.

Provide superior customer service to internal and external customers

Perform other duties as assigned or as necessary

TYPICAL PHYSICAL DEMANDS: Employees must be able to physically access all exterior and interior parts of the community and amenities. Some bending, stooping and stretching. Occasionally lifting items weighing up to 30 lbs. Requires eye-hand coordination and manual dexterity sufficient to operate a keyboard, photocopier, telephone, calculator, camera and other office equipment. Requires normal range of hearing and eyesight to record, prepare, and communicate appropriate reports and publications. Extensive data input and required ability to sit for long periods of time. This position may require an ability to stand during special events.

TYPICAL WORKING CONDITIONS: Normal office environment open Monday through Friday from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. Flexible schedules with occasional evening and weekend work.
