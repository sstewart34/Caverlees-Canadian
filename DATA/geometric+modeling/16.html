About the Job

Product Design Engineer (ESD1012)

Company Description:

Magpul Industries Corp. (Magpul) is an innovative design and manufacturing company of firearms accessories for police, military and shooting enthusiasts. Magpul was founded in 1999 with the intent of developing a simple device to aid in the manipulation of rifle magazines while reloading under stress. The companys name comes from this original product called the Magpul. Over the last decade Magpul has continued to grow and develop using much the same mission and process with a focus on innovation, simplicity, and efficiency. As a growing industry leader, Magpul is in constant need of talented people who share our vision and passion for our products and our industry.

Job Description:

Magpul Industries Corp. is seeking a Product Design Engineer who has a passion for robust and creative product design and the ability to work as part of a team within the design group.

Specific Duties and Responsibilities:

New Product Design & Validation: Works within the Design Department to advise on core engineering aspects during the development of new products. Major objectives include: 1) Reviewing and analyzing engineering designs for functionality, durability and manufacturability, 2) Reviewing engineering documentation for completeness and clarity 3) Assisting designers with design concepts to meet new product specifications, 4) providing recommendations to optimize designs, 5) Working closely with the data management group to produce the projects technical data package (TDP) including establishing Bills of Material and critical design components tolerances 6) Developing and conducting lab experiments to validate designs, 7) Evaluating and qualifying prototypes, first-article samples, new suppliers and manufacturing processes, 8) Advising on assembly tooling and fixtures, and 9) Working closely with the manufacturing group to facilitate the transition of new products from design into manufacturing.

General Support: Promote Magpul Foundations and enforce internal policies, procedures, goals, objectives, and good manufacturing practices.

Other Duties: Perform other duties as assigned.

Education and Experience Requirements:

Education: BS Mechanical Engineering from an accredited college.

Experience: Applied Design Engineering with 5+ years experience.

Job Knowledge, Skill and Ability Requirements:

Communication: Must have proven skills and be an excellent verbal and written communicator.

Design: Proven track record of consumer product or related design experience with extreme attention to detail. Design of polymer-based consumer products is desirable.

  • Mechanisms: Experience in mechanism and mechanical system design with strong kinematic background.
  • Manufacturing Engineering: Broad spectrum knowledge of manufacturing concepts with knowledge and experience of plastic injection molding. An advanced specialization in plastic injection molding is desirable.
  • CAD/CAE: Must be proficient with solid modeling and finite element analysis (FEA) software packages. SolidWorks and COSMOS knowledge is preferable.
  • TDP: Ability to read, analyze, interpret and correct mechanical drawings including tolerancing, Geometric Dimensioning & Tolerancing (GD&T) and related engineering specifications.
  • Mechanical Aptitude: Very strong, demonstrated mechanical aptitude required.
  • Time Mgmt: Ability to prioritize multiple projects.
  • Adaptability: Must be proficient in learning new concepts while working in a highly dynamic environment. Must be able to seamlessly work across boundaries and interact with management, factory personnel, other engineers, suppliers and customers.

Attitude: Enthusiastic, cooperative, and positive behavior with solution-oriented focus.

Leadership: Lead engineer experience is a plus.

  • Weapon Systems Knowledge: Must be a firearms enthusiast. Working knowledge of firearms and related systems is highly preferable.

Job Knowledge, Skill and Ability Requirements:

Organizational: This position reports to the Design Manager

Salary: Competitive salary commensurate to experience.

Benefits: Benefits include medical, dental, vision, life and long-term disability insurance as well as voluntary short-term disability and accident insurance.

Background: Must be eligible to work in the United States for any employer. The applicant must be authorized to receive Technical Data and/or Technology under U.S. export control laws. Any offer made is contingent on the applicant passing a background check that will include a restricted party screening and, for prospective employees who are Foreign Persons, on the Company obtaining all necessary export licenses. In addition the applicant will be required to pass a criminal background check and drug screening. Active or former security clearance is a plus.
