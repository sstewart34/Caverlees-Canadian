About the Job

Principal Engineer, Systems Applications

SUMMARY

Acting as the assigned team focal, and with the minimal supervision, this individual is responsible for defining, documenting, and reviewing all requirements for assigned application development projects and the coordination with software development and test teams. Duties include the creation and accountability for the up-keep of development schedules, including resource plans, monitoring engineering development budgets, monitoring and reporting potential risks and providing regular status reports to the engineering management team for all assigned applications. The Principle Systems Applications Engineer is expected to establish and lead the following standard practices and procedures, ensure that all requisite documentation is developed and reviewed with peer groups and stakeholders. The Principle Systems Application Engineer is also expected to work closely with the Product Line Manager, Software team or 3rd party software vendor for each assigned application to ensure that the application that is being developed meets product requirements and take required actions should the team deviate. The Principle System Applications engineer is expected to work with minimal supervision, drive development activities and take accountability for associated scope, schedule and budgets and provide direction and leadership within the development team; being able to make informed decisions relating to their development responsibilities. The Principle System Applications Engineer is also expected to have a good feel for when issues need to be escalated and do so in a timely manner providing management the right level of clear information required to assess a situation and make informed decisions.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES .

*Works closely with Applications Product Line Management team, customer team and media services managers to fully understand product requirements and takes the lead in developing creative & robust solutions to business and technical problems ensuring the developed application meets all of the original product requirements; inc. accountability for budget & schedule

*Leads all the activities related to definition, design, documentation, and support required to ship high quality applications to airline customers throughout the development cycle:

-Analyzes requirements coming from the aircraft manufacture/customer

-Defines and designs the applications architecture and data flow throughout all aspects of

the system

-Develops requirement documentation and coordinates technical/peer reviews

-Monitors implementation activity in coordination with the software department and/or

subcontractors

-Establishes development schedule & budget and oversees the development cycle from

kick-off to generic release

-Monitoring development & implementation activity in coordination with the software

department and/or subcontractors ensuring development is on schedule and per budget

-Development of SOW for 3rd part contractors

-Applications system design. This includes system-level requirements definition, system

architecture analysis through modeling and simulation and the generation of respective

documentation in coordination with the Applications Program Manager or the Product

Policy Manager

-Systems integration and system verification, troubleshooting & test. This includes the

coordination of system level test plans for sub-system and system-level integration testing,

perform the duties of a director of system-level testing, and oversee the generation of the

system test report

*Responsible for planning, coordination and organization of each phase of the project from kick-off to completion; utilizes schedules to organize and understand project tasks and manages the tasks throughout the system design, development and verification phases taking responsibility for providing direction to the team and having accountability for schedule and budgets

*Holds accountability for budgetary status of program against plan and future work load; EACs & ETCs

*Identifies and resolves potential problems before they occur therefore minimizing the impact to schedule and cost. Raises issues to management in timely manner and makes recommendations towards resolution

*Manages, documents, and ensures product compliance with customer requirements

*Generates documentation for each respective phase. The definition phase includes the following documentation: Systems Requirements Specification, System Design Documentation, Inferface Control Documentation, Modeling & Simulation Documentation

*Exercises a very high level of initiative and independent judgment in coordinating the work of peer personnel within the group and external groups as well

*Proposes process changes to improve the efficiency and quality of work. Takes on independent projects to help improve team/process efficiency and quality

*Manages material resources (HW or SW) as needed for system-level testing

*In post-delivery support, assists with problem isolation/identification and may have to provide on-site support, conduct system design reviews and actively participate in hardware/software design reviews

*Performs other duties and leading other tasks as assigned and may be required to travel domestically and internationally
