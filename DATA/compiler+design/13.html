About the Job

Database Developer

Job Description

Role: Database Developer

Location: Downtown Minneapolis, MN

Length: Full-time permanent on-site W2

Salary Range:  Based on experience and qualifications

 

You must be authorized to work in the United States on a full-time basis.

Required skills

       4+ years of development experience with SQL and stored procedures within an RDBMS

       4+ years of development experience with Microsoft's T-SQL (ad hoc, scripted and stored procedures) 

       4+ years of development experience with an applicable programming language such as PHP or Java

       4+ years of development experience with Linux / Unix shell and Windows

       4+ years of experience with data transfer such as SFTP and NDM

       2+ years of experience with MS SQL Server - Administration and/or SSIS

       2+ years of experience with data transformation methods and tools like ETL

       Experience with multiple version control systems

       Demonstrated experience with multiple databases and environments

       Intermediate understanding of code best practices

       Proficiency writing development documentation for many audiences

       Ability to work in a team-based, open development environment

       Capacity to analyze software specifications from business requirements and present quality deliverables on schedule consistently

       Excellent analytical, interpersonal, and written communication skills

Duties and Responsibilities

       Assists and occasionally leads in the intermediate level design, coding, and testing of technical solutions using  SQL, T-SQL, stored procedures, and multiple programming languages within the database environments

       Creates and maintains data downloads and processing in support of the application using data transfer utilities, programming languages, and database environments

       In depth understanding of standard systems development lifecycle processes and applies these standards within the organization's software development methodology

       Applies intermediate to advanced knowledge of industry trends and developments to improve service to our clients

       Identifies system deficiencies and recommends solutions, taking a proactive approach to software development

       Provides assistance in meeting development deadlines and schedules, often solely responsible for non-critical work

       Recognizes the necessity of and adheres to established coding standards

       Grasps project and development plans and is able to clearly articulate roles, project goals, and timelines

       Occasionally works with project managers to interpret business requirements and offer technical solutions

       Intermediate level planning, estimation, documentation, and status reporting

       Reviews, evaluates, designs, implements, and maintains the database environments

       Mentor and train other developers

Education

Bachelors degree with coursework in Computer Science or Management Information Systems. Candidates with equivalent job-related experience in a position with similar responsibilities will also be considered.
