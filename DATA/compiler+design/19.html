About the Job

Company: Mentor Graphics
Job Title: Corporate Application Engineer - Place and Route
Job Location: US - CA - Fremont
Job Category: Applications Engineering



Mentor Graphics Corporation (MENT) is a global technology leader in Electronic Design Automation, providing software and hardware design solutions that help engineers around the world create new and innovative products. Each year, our customers use our tools to push the boundaries of technology in order to deliver smaller, faster and more reliable products that run the world's high tech devices.



Position Overview
Work with customers as a world leader in electronic software design solutions, providing services for the world's largest electronics and semiconductor companies. Continue to learn and support state of the art, Mentor Graphics place and route tools. In this position, you will have very close involvement with customers, software engineering and product marketing. You will become a member of a team of highly motivated individuals committed to providing award-winning technical support to customers and field personnel using Mentor Graphics Olympus SOC. You will work closely with the customer to understand their current and future needs, as well as ensuring successful deployment of Mentor Graphics Olympus SOC in their design flow.


Responsibilities include providing in-depth technical support and training to our customers, staying up to date with new capabilities, working with customers on issues they may experience with Mentor Graphics Olympus SOC. As a team member, you will focus on customer advocacy and product supportability to influence product quality and direction. Capture and share problem resolutions in application notes, provide input to product documentation and training materials, reproduce customers' problems and provide resolution or workaround, and provide well documented Defect Reports.



Job Qualifications
The successful candidate will possess the following combination of education and experience:
BSEE (or equivalent experience) with 5 or more years of demonstrated experience in semiconductor layout of large designs in state-of-the-art processes.
Preferred candidates will have experience in complex state-of-the-art place-and-route experience, VLSI, digital design, clock-tree and timing analysis, and basic Tcl/Perl programming.
Effective communication skills, written and verbal. The position will involve direct interaction with the customer as well as internally with appropriate disciplines of QA, Marketing, Product Engineering, Development Engineering, Documentation and Training.
An ability to work in a diverse "global" environment.
Experience with one industry standard Place and Route tool is required [Mentor (Olympus-SoC), Synopsys (IC Compiler, Physical Compiler), Cadence (SoC Encounter), or Magma (Talus, BlastFusion)].
Experience with Perl, TCL and UNIX scripting are a plus.
Technical applications engineering or customer support experience is also beneficial.
The successful candidate will have the ability to work independently, will be self-motivated, have excellent interpersonal skills and technical curiosity and motivation to learn and succeed, as well as leadership traits to ensure customer success.
Customer sensitivity, the ability to multiplex many issues, and the desire to help customers exploit new technologies are essential for success in the position.
Some travel may be required.



Work - Life Balance
Mentor Graphics believes in fostering a work environment that promotes a healthy work-life balance. Our world class benefits package includes up to 32 days of vacation/holiday pay per year, 401k matching, Stock purchase plan, annual performance reviews/bonuses, education reimbursement, partially paid Medical/Dental/Vision insurance and much more.
