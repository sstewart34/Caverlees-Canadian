About the Job

CLINICAL RESEARCH ASSOCIATE

FOR DERMATOLOGY RESEARCH

 

Job Description:

             

Academic physician with a busy practice in medical dermatology has a large volume of clinically relevant data. Data consists of clinical profiles, treatment regimens and follow up.  Many patients with only mucosal diseases. Seeking a physician who can assist in data collection, literature review, assimilation, analysis, and preparation of manuscripts.  Individual will be co-author on manuscripts based on their involvement and contributions.  Presentations at local and national meetings are possible.  Work will include collaboration with physicians, scientists and other institutions, locally and nationally. Previous experience is not essential.   The research is not industry-sponsored and does not include drug trials.  Position is located in Boston.

Position is ideal for:

o       Foreign medical graduates to get publication prior to residency application

o       Physicians wanting to change their specialty to dermatology

o       Medical students wanting to get into dermatology residency programs

o       Physicians leaving the armed forces or public health service and indecisive as to their future career paths. 

 

Requirements:

         Education:  M.D. or D.O.

         Speak, read, and write English very well.

         Mandatory:  Must be a U.S. Citizenship or possess U.S. Green Card / Work Authorization  We cannot provide U.S. Visa Sponsorship

         Digital photography skills necessary to take photographs of diseased patients

         Excellent computer skills, especially in PubMed, word processing, Photoshop, PowerPoint, and others.

         Organized, methodical and systematic worker

         Individual must have demonstrated reliability, responsibility, dependability, and capacity to work independently

         Must be a team player and willing to work with other staff members when needed.

         Previous publications would be an asset, but not an essential requirement.

 

Availability:

              Position currently open - Willing to wait if candidate needs time to join.

 

Remuneration:

         Modest stipend negotiable, based on experience, training and productivity.

 

Please submit resume and the names of 3 references, with their contact information, that can attest to your abilities.
