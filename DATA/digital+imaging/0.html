Interested in an outside sales position with a dynamic digital imaging company? Consider a career with Phase One!

 

Phase One is the worlds leader in open-platform based medium format camera systems and solutions. Phase One medium format cameras, digital backs and lenses are designed to deliver superior quality image capture and investment value. Phase Ones Capture One software helps streamline capture and post-production processes for both medium format and DSLR cameras.

 

Phase One is seeking candidates to fill a full-time, entry-level sales position covering the Southern California area. We are looking for a dynamic, service oriented individual who thrives on the cutting-edge digital imaging industry.

 

Responsibilities include:

     Lead generation, follow up and proposal generation

     Presentation of hardware, software and workflow solutions to professional photographers, studio

          managers and studio owners

     Presentation of hardware and software to large groups during events

     Set up and use of still camera equipment including studio lighting

     Installation of hardware and software including setup and training

     Support and maintain a strong relationship with Phase One partners

 

Qualifications:

     MUST be VERY personable, energetic & willing to learn

     Outside sales experience a plus

     Basic MAC computer skills a MUST, PC experience a plus

     Medium & large format digital studio photography experience a plus

     Experience using Capture One software a plus

     Basic Photoshop, Excel and Word experience a plus

     MUST have own, reliable transportation, position also requires overnight air travel

     MUST pass factory technical training course

     MUST be based in the Los Angeles area

 

Phase One is an employee-owned company based in Copenhagen with offices in New York, London,

Tokyo, Cologne and Shanghai. Phase One companies include Leaf-Imaging and Mamiya Camera company.

 

Compensation package (including benefits) commensurate with experience.

 

Please send your resume to Murray Elliott:  mje@phaseone.com
