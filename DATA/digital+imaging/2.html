About the Job

About us:

Charles & Colvard, Ltd., based in the Research Triangle Park area of North Carolina, is the global sole source of moissanite, a unique, near-colorless created gemstone that is distinct from other gemstones and jewels based on its exceptional fire, brilliance, luster, durability, and rarity. Charles & Colvard Created Moissanite is currently incorporated into fine jewelry sold through domestic and international retailers and other sales channels. Charles & Colvard, Ltd. is headquartered in Morrisville, North Carolina, and its common stock is listed on the NASDAQ Global Select Market under the symbol "CTHR". For more information, please access www.charlesandcolvard.com.

Description:

Charles & Colvard, a gemstone manufacturer and jewelry retail company located in Morrisville North Carolina is seeking an enthusiastic, self-motivated, high end and experienced product photographer with a minimum of 5 years experience. Jewelry and small product lighting, image capture, Jewelry photography, retouching, set building, styling and database management skills are necessary for this position. This individual must be able to work in a fast paced, challenging environment.

The photographer/digital imaging specialist will work closely with marketing initiatives which include producing images for our website, collateral, catalogs, social media and more. This is an extremely hands-on position. Candidate must have the ability to see an idea through from start to finish. Must have strong retouching, and graphic design skills. Must have proficiencies as a jewelry photographer with fine-tuned lighting skills and solid raw workflow practices. Videography and video editing skills a plus.

Responsibilities:

Proper file naming skills for organization's image database

Monitoring of image capture process and raw workflow ensuring that all images are captured properly.

Communicate with Marketing, Creative, IT, Merchandising to facilitate proper workflow between teams.

Deliver files to web, production, merchandising, database and client servers.

Raw conversion to deliverable file formats

Collecting copy and graphics for website collateral

Photographer for in-house jewelry product and staff

Working with our CRM system or similar database management systems.

Requirements

Education:

Bachelors degree in commercial photography with at least 5 years of professional experience.

Computer skills:

Knowledge of data backup principals

Proficient in Microsoft Office Products

Web and Print design experience using Adobe Creative Suite 5.5

Experience in RAW file types and color management workflow

Experience:

Design Skills:

Photoshop- Advanced working knowledge and must have experience processing from RAW to PSD, TIF, and JPG etc...

Color Management- must have a working knowledge of color profiling.

Color Theory- must have a solid understanding color, color space, color corrections, color conversions within a wide array of photo processes.

Digital capture management- daily management of image archives.

Creativity and Innovation- Exceptional problem solving skills, creative color layout and photo composting a plus.

Workflow & Communication- Project management from RAW to final output; we are looking for someone who can take care of a project on their own and communicate effectively with our in-house marketing team.

Other Skills:

Ability to work closely with others and be part of a team experience

Videography a plus

Light video editing skills a plus

Ability to work under pressure to exacting standards and deadlines

Qualities:

Fast and efficient without compromising the integrity of the image.

Attention to detail and process.

There is no relocation assistance for this position.
