About the Job

 
 


CORE JAVA DEVELOPER


Are you an innovative Java Developer that wants to get on the ground floor of a disruptive technology? Would you like to develop a massively scalable and high-throughput distributed system that provides insightful analytics that power multi-billion dollar decision making? Are you looking to make true impact to the bottom line of a growing new company?
Our Direct client is building the next generation analytics platform for hedge funds using the latest in distributed computing and data visualization. The firm distills the subtle signals in large datasets to highlight the strengths and weaknesses of investment decisions, guiding investors to better results.
The company is growing rapidly and is looking to expand their Back End Engineering team with the industrys top talent. This is a young product, offering an opportunity to shape the core platform while learning the ins and outs of hedge fund investing.
The firm approaches finance with a fresh perspective, relying on the best in open source, computational algorithms, big data analysis, functional programming, and distributed computing. You will join a highly skilled team of passionate, innovative, and intellectually curious engineers that come from diverse backgrounds and share a common thirst for growth, impact by innovation, and open source participation.. The environment is highly collaborative and multi-disciplinary.
 
Responsibilities
 

  • Take ownership of various initiatives to build client solutions, new products, and internal tools
  • Architect and develop core platform infrastructure to scale with the growth of users, products, and data
  • Investigate, select, and implement various infrastructure libraries for distributed caching and computing
  • Work across the development stack to build various products driven by R&D, CEO, and Product Owners
  • Collaborate with Front End development team to implement business logic based on UX needs
  •  Become an expert in Scala, Mongo DB and various open source libraries we use
  •  Build reusable and clean code and ensure quality through thorough unit and regression tests

 
 
Qualifications
 

  • A Computer Science degree from a top university program (Undergraduate or Graduate)
  • 2 + years of experience working with complex Core Java or Scala Enterprise Systems
  • Deep knowledge of the JVM, Core Java (5,6,7) and general development toolsets
  • Serious experience with building high traffic, scalable and high performance systems
  • Strong appreciation for architecture, problem solving, testing and agile development
  • Aptitude and interest in distributed computing and caching paradigms
  • Rudimentary skills using SQL and an understanding of various NOSQL offerings (Mongo DB)
  • Experience in the following is a plus but not required: Scala, SQL, Mongo, NoSQL, Open Source

 
 
Personal Traits
 

  • High enthusiasm, high integrity, and low ego; the focus is on a strong team rather than individual stars
  • Creative thinker able to solve complex problems with inventive solutions
  • Must enjoy a fast-paced, results oriented environment with an agile product life-cycle
  • Aptitude for flexible component based architecture and clean design while remaining pragmatic
  • Driven strategic thinker with ability to take a product from inception to release
  • Clear verbal and written communication, attention to detail, and high level of professionalism
  • Desire to work across the development stack and learn technologies outside of core competency
  • Desire to pursue an deeper education in finance through courses, CFA, CAIA or MBA program
