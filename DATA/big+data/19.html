About the Job

Deputy DirectorMarket Research - Women's Health:

My client, a large pharmaceutical company based in northern New Jersey, takes pride in knowing that their work is an affirmation of life. It stems from their commitment to advance patient care and to shape the future of medicine. It also connects them to the many lives they serve in their therapeutic areas of expertise, including Women's Health. Job description

Manages and directs Marketing Intelligence (Market Research) projects that lead to market driven insights for the Women's Health Care brands. Responsible to develop and implement a Market Research Brand plan that drives, informs and enhances decision making

Guide and direct Associate Directors in the integration of Primary and Secondary market insights, ensures actionable interpretation and presentation of knowledge on a timely basis for strategic and tactical decisions

Represents the Voice of the Customer (Patient, Payor and Physician) for the Brand team and to Business Unit head

Brings a strong business orientation to research design

Identifies and integrates all Internal and External Market Research resources (Primary, Secondary, Marketing Sciences, Data Warehousing, etc.) to achieve Brand goals and objectives

Provide context for forecasting assumptions for the in-line brands

Major Tasks of Position

Manages Market Intelligence support and needs at the brand levels

Direct development and then manage implementation of an integrated MI plan and timely execution against the plan

Maintains constant market surveillance and alerts Brand Management, BU Head and others to changes in market conditions, shifts in trends and factors that could impact brand performance

Ensures that market data is available, understood, analyzed and action plans can be based on and supported by reliable market data/analyses

Manage Brand budget for all Market Research

Implements vendor strategies

Utilizes best practices
ii. Generates the best value and price

Qualifications

Bachelors required; Masters Degree ( Quantitative Analysis, Social Science, MBA) highly preferred

10+ Years of Marketing Research experience

Direct report management experience is required

Depth and breadth of market research experience

Highly motivated at the cutting edge of the utilization of data, tools and techniques to understand product/market drivers and performance variables and the ways to measure them

Womens Health experience a plus

Ability to lead efforts to understand customers and market and to represent this knowledge accurately and powerfully to advantage Brand in planning Brand strategy and tactics

Proactive

Solid understanding of Primary and Secondary data sources and methods and practices

Utilizes and often innovates adapted methods that reflect best practices

Strong skills in generating insights drawn from Primary and Secondary data sources and other sources of information (eg. Internal sales and other databases)

Extremely strong communication and leadership skills
