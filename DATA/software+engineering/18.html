About the Job

Strong preference to be in Seattle, WA or Tucson, AZ.

OpenText Enterprise Information Management (EIM) technologies and business solutions allow organizations to take full advantage of enterprise information to gain better business insight, capitalize on opportunities to positively impact the business, improve process velocity, reduce risks related to information governance, and protect sensitive information and intellectual property from internal leaks and external threats. With growing volumes and a host of formats to manage and leverage, organizations need to bring structure to the unstructured. By doing so, they will be unleashing the power of information to drive faster decision making, improved agility, strong security policies, and an increased ability to both exploit the opportunities and control the risks of enterprise information.

OpenText provides solutions across the entire range of core EIM capabilities sophisticated, secure, high-value, and cost-effective onsite, via mobile devices, private cloud, or in the cloud.

The Open Text Fax and Document Distribution Group (FDDG), the worlds leading fax solution provider, is hiring a Director of Sales Engineering to recruit, develop and manage the Sales Engineering team, ensures optimal resource allocation and assumes a leadership role in the organization.  In addition they should be technically experienced with the technologies their team supports and be able to step in to a sales engineer role if needed.  The Director of Sales Engineering consists of both an individual contributor and management role.

Individual Contributor Role
Step into the SE role to support sales calls and product demonstrations as needed.  Implement software as needed in support of professional services, evaluations, proof of concept and integration testing.
Constantly learn and develop expertise in the technologies relevant to the Fax and Document Delivery Group.
Provide input to product marketing and management on future direction and enhancements to our products.
Review and contribute to documentation efforts.
Create and give presentations that effectively position Open Text FDDG products to solve tangible business issues plaguing the prospect or customer in coordination with the Sales resource assigned to the account
Maintain regular communication with other teams in the organization, especially sales, product marketing, product management and support.
Attend meetings as needed.
Track activity in SalesForce.com

Management Role
Recruit and develop the highest caliber Sales Engineers.  Development includes ensuring all Sales Engineers are as ready, as knowledgeable and as equipped as possible to perform their duties in support of sales and the greater organization.  Management includes ensuring all tasks are completed and performed at the highest level of quality and that the Sales Engineers have continuous opportunity for growth and development.  Utilize the performance review system (Success Factors) to continually provide guidance on performance and development of Sales Engineers.  
Resource Allocation.  Make sure Sales Engineers are trained in the core products and available to support the sales team and greater organization.  Ensure the Sales Engineers have the resources needed to perform their role and maintain a balance between pre and post sales activities and special projects.  Prioritize product skill sets based on observed demand and percent of sales revenue generated along with guidance from management.
Sales Engineering Leadership.  Be personally involved in the most important sales opportunities to help the Sales Engineer have the most success.  The Manager, Sales Engineering role demands individuals who are leaders, and who remain positive in the face of adversity.   All of the above activities should be conducted with the following traits:  strong leadership ability and experience, proven or demonstrably outstanding presentation, speaking and writing skills, ability to articulate complex topics to novice or technical audience, ability to translate technical advantages into business benefits, and polished and professional in both appearance, personal interaction, and all communications.
Create reports in SalesForce.com to better evaluate and manage the teams performance and help guide the direction of their effort.
Perform administrative tasks like ensuring team members schedule their personal time off and follow corporate policies on travel and expenses.



Knowledge, Skills and Experience:

Successful candidate must have 10+ years demonstrated ability and proven success in Sales Engineering roles in a high tech environment.
5+ years success in the planning, development and launch of Sales Engineering strategies of packaged software products a plus.
Experience with the financial services, healthcare and document management markets a plus.
Ability to conceive, produce and implement technical sales tools that help position Open Text Fax and Document Delivery products within our partner and customer base
Must have executive level communication skills and the ability to effectively represent complex ideas in a simple, easy to understand manner to a range of audiences. This includes effective written and speaking skills.


Travel requirements - 35%


Education:

Relevant bachelors degree and / or experience
