About the Job

K12 is a dynamic company on a mission to provide the most compelling, comprehensive, and effective K-12 education available. Our employees are a critical part of an organization that is providing powerful, new options for the way children can be educated. They have a passion for education and a drive to make a difference. We pride ourselves on maintaining the highest level of integrity. We have the same high expectations for our employees as we do for the students who are in our programs.
K12 is seeking a talented individual to join our Systems Development team as a Principal Software Engineer. If you are a motivated, team-oriented individual with experience in developing software applications, we have a challenging and rewarding position for you. The Principal Software Engineer takes an ownership role in providing technical leadership to members of the Platform Development team by performing full-cycle software development and creating high quality solution designs which address application architectural concerns. The Principal Software Engineer coordinates closely with members of the Software Product Development Architecture team as well as other Platform Development groups to successfully drive complex technical designs across system domain boundaries to build enterprise class, high performance, highly scalable industry leading online education systems.

Essential Functions, Duties and Responsibilities:

Designs solutions that align with business blueprints, delivery constraints and infrastructure standards as applicable;
Responsible for macro scale architecture of a software technology and applications, and providing technical guidance, direction and oversight to multiple development teams working on different initiatives, products or technologies to ensure that the technologies interoperate or connect to each other appropriately;
Works in partnership with the infrastructure and development teams, as well as the business community to recommend and integrate different approaches to develop or enhance advanced systems for the company;
Provides technical leadership and expertise on highly complex software systems to develop cutting-edge solutions, proactively bringing new ideas, technologies, and methodologies to the team;
Prototype, research and recommend new technologies, approaches, and product features, including making them available to other engineers as framework or components;
Deep dive into the current platform internals, and recommend potential improvement areas and come up with technical solutions to make those improvements;
Accountable for the delivery of design solutions that promote flexibility, scalability, high performance, efficiency and improve current techniques;
Ensure the logical and systematic conversion of customer or product requirements into total systems solutions that acknowledge technical, schedule, and cost constraints;
Ensures implementation of processes and the optimal application of technology and engineering resources to meet product development and quality requirements;
Stay up to date and upgraded on the latest developments in the software field and strategies to effectively apply them to improve k12 software systems;
Research, prepare and present technology proposals and white papers, tailored to audience needs;
Assist in establishing best practices and standards for information systems procedures;
Assist in technical audits and make sure to implement audit recommendations;
Leads, mentors, guides and reviews the work of other software engineers;
Maintains strong verbal and written communication, collaboration, analysis and problem solving skills and is proficient at planning and organizing, capable of working under pressure and within tight deadlines;
Work with geographically distributed development teams;
Other duties as assigned.
Competencies: To perform the job successfully, an individual should demonstrate the following competencies.

Adaptability/Flexibility: Adapts to change, is open to new ideas, takes on new responsibilities, handles pressure, and adjusts plans to meet changing needs.
Integrity/Ethics: Deals with others in a straightforward and honest manner, is accountable for actions, maintains confidentiality, supports company values, and conveys good news and bad.
Teamwork: Meets all team deadlines and responsibilities, listens to others and values opinions, helps team leader to meet goals, welcomes newcomers and promotes a team atmosphere.

Supervisory Responsibilities: This position has no formal supervisory responsibilities.



Requirements:

Minimum Required Qualifications:

Bachelors degree in Computer Science or related field AND
Ten (10) years of experience in software development with eight (8) of those developing web applications and two (2) in a technical leadership role OR
Equivalent combination of education and experience

Requirements:

Java, WebObjects J2EE
ANT, Unix, Integrated Development Environments (IDE) such as Eclipse and NetBeans
Revision control tools such as Git, Subversion or StarTeam
Experience with database development: SQL, Oracle 9i or higher, MS SQL Server, Java Database Connectivity (JDBC), CASE Studio
Experience with web application servers, e.g. Tomcat, Apache, Weblogic, JBoss
Experience with Service Oriented Architecture (SOA) and Object Oriented Design Patterns and Refactoring
Experience developing web applications using frameworks like Spring, Seam, Struts, etc.
Experience with persistence frameworks like EJB3, Hibernate, JPA, Toplink, etc.
Experience with presentation tier frameworks such as Ajax, Ext-JS, Dojo, Spry, GWT, etc.
Experience with some of the following user interface (UI) technologies: Flash, JSP, Struts, Object Oriented JavaScript, XML, advanced HTML & CSS
Experience with software architecture frameworks such as TOGAF, MDA, IAF, etc.
Iterative and incremental software methodology experience such as Agile, Spiral, XP, RUP, etc.
Iterative and incremental software methodology certification such as Certified Scrum Master, RUP Certified Solution Designer, DSDM Practitioner, etc.
Experience with cross browser support, code optimization and performance tuning
Experience with any of the following is a significant plus: Oracle BI, jBPM, iText, Simple Object Access Protocol (SOAP), Simple Mail Transfer Protocol (SMTP)
Experience and passion for troubleshooting issues in a web application environment (e.g. Tomcat, Oracle, NAS, load balancer)
Experience with internationalization and accessibility
Highly motivated with a drive to be continuously learning
Strong written and verbal communication skills
Ability to lead and work as part of a team
