About the Job

VIZ Media is looking for an Animation Marketing Manager to join our Animation team!

  • Demonstrated past success in ownership of marketing campaigns from design to execution to reporting A MUST
  • Strong presentation and public speaking skills A MUST
  • Strong analytical framework and skill set A MUST
  • Ability to review & write copy STRONGLY PREFERRED
  • Knowledge of consumer entertainment such as anime, manga, games and related merchandise and some exposure to entertainment media (comics, books, video) STRONGLY PREFERRED
  • Professional understanding of the entertainment market targeted at teen boys and 18 34 males STRONGLY PREFERRED


SUMMARY

The Animation Marketing Manager plans, organizes, and manages consumer facing marketing campaigns for all VIZ Media animation product categories (DVD & Digital) with a strict emphasis on ROI.


PRINCIPAL DUTIES
  1. Responsible for the planning and execution of consumer facing marketing programs designed to drive sales of VIZ Medias animation products (DVD and Digital)
  2. Responsible for developing, executing, & maintaining a marketing strategy and presence in the social media space (i.e. Facebook, Twitter, etc.).
  3. Represents VIZ Media animation products, including speaking on panels and participating in interviews
  4. Works closely with the VP of Animation to manage annual marketing budgets and spends
  5. Responsible for developing and implementing high ROI online marketing campaigns leveraging social media and other online marketing vehicles
  6. Monitors, reviews, and optimizes campaigns by developing in-depth understanding of key performance drivers
  7. Manages day-to-day progress of marketing planning and execution
  8. Maintains brand integrity across animation product lines, advertising, and all other internal and external communication
  9. Collaborates with a wide variety of functional areas including: sales, production, creative, and operations to execute specific marketing plans for each assigned brand
  10. Prepares marketing objectives and schedules for all phases of product solicitation and introduction to market, including but not limited to sales materials and retail presentations.
  11. Conducts market research and identifies and tracks market trends in company's industry
  12. Manages 3rd party relationships with external marketing partners
  13. Produces competitive analysis materials comparing product with its key competitors
  14. Develops monthly, quarterly, & annual ROI reports for senior management
  15. Participates in key sales and marketing presentations

In addition to these responsibilities, performs other activities as assigned

  • Ability to work legally in the United States

This position does not supervise others, but may give guidance to the work of interns and freelancers.

Approximately 90% of time spent on the job involves use of a video display terminal; therefore, must be able to sit for extended periods and to utilize standard ergonomics practices. Primarily works in an office environment, requiring the following activity: lifting and carrying materials weighing up to 15 pounds, kneeling, bending. Must be able to use standard office equipment such as copiers and faxes. MUST be able to travel both domestically and internationally as needed.

As part of a multi-media entertainment company, employees may be subjected to work-related images including content such as: sexually explicit images, nudity, and graphic violence. Employees should be willing to work with this type of content. VIZ Media does not condone the viewing of this content where not work-related.
