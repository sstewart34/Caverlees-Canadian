About the Job

BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE ARCHITECT

BI Architects envision, develop and support strategic operational business Intelligence to provide the right information to the right people at the right time. The Business Intelligence Data Architect is responsible for designing and implementing BI Software and systems, including ensuring successful integration with databases and data warehouses. Related activities include:  selecting, blueprinting, gathering requirements, designing, and rolling out BI solutions to end users.
 As part of the BI management team, the BI Architect's responsibilities also include managing direct reports, mentoring other team members, educating clients and prospects in the latest technology developments in BI, and  supporting the growth of the Business Intelligence Practice.

SPECIFIC RESPONSIBILITIES
The Data Architect is responsible for the day-to-day management and delivery of Business intelligence projects to our clients, who span a diverse range of industries, products and services. Primary responsibilities include the following:
 Guide clients in developing and implementing BI-related strategic goals that will assist them in achieving their business objectives. 
 Direct, organize, and lead projects in the implementation and use of new BI software tools and systems.
 Lead the integration efforts for merging BI platforms with enterprise systems and applications.
 Design, code, test, and document all new or modified BI systems, applications, and programs.
 Develop the semantic layer, metadata, reports, and report definitions.
 Lead the design of databases and data warehouses to ensure interoperability with BI solutions.
 Analyze user requirements and, based on findings, design functional specifications for BI front-end applications.
 Work with project managers to ensure that data entry, retrieval, change and delete functions meet business requirements for project completion.
 Perform job duties according to the organization's business systems development methodology and/or its Systems Development Life Cycle (SDLC) and Agile methodologies.
 Design and deliver end-user training and training materials; provide technical support as necessary.
 Troubleshoot BI tools, systems, and software; performance-tune these applications as necessary.
 Act as evangelist for BI benefits across the organization; promote BI usage to relevant clients and prospects.
 Evaluate and select database/data warehouse components, including hardware, relational database management systems, ETL software, metadata management tools, and database design solutions.
 Conduct research and provide recommendations regarding BI products, services, and standards to support client procurement and development efforts.
 Mentor and/or provide leadership to junior members of the team.
 Develop, design, implement, enhance, maintain and analyze database systems, queries, and procedures in SQL Server 200 to 2008 environments.
 Develop complex SQL 2008 SSIS packages for ETL data to Data Warehouse projects and assignments and be able to modify existing Data Warehouse structure (SQL Server various versions) to incorporate data from different data sources.
 Create ETL design specifications
  Perform development and unit testing of ETL processes
 Assist client personnel in migration of ETL components to test environment
 Participate in integration testing of solution that involves database, OLAP and reporting components in addition to ETL
 Assist client personnel in execution of acceptance testing and perform bug tracking and resolution of ETL defects as necessary
 Perform initial data load and data validation in production environment
 Conduct knowledge transfer sessions with client personnel
 Facilitate user requirement discussions with the business and drive architecture and design

REQUIRED EDUCATION
 Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, Information Systems, or Computer Engineering with a focus on Data Warehousing.  Masters degree a plus.
 Microsoft Certified Information Technology Professional -MCITP Certification in Business Intelligence

REQUIRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE
 7+ years data warehousing experience
 5+ years with Microsoft SSIS/DTS
 Minimum of 4 years system integration experience, including hands-on experience in at least 4 of the following tasks:
 Developing strategies and data flow diagrams showing data interaction between various systems
 Data lifecycle modeling
 Working with BI/ETL/Reporting tools and processes
 Data conversion planning
 Data conversion testing
 Knowledge of full life cycle development methodologies
 Developing capacity plans for new and existing systems
 Performance testing and tuning databases and/or data access components
 Previous experience as a DBA on any major RDBMS (DB2, Oracle, SQL Server, MySQL)
 Hands-on experience with some ETL,(Extract-Transform-Load) commercial Software Products (e.g Informatica, Datastage, MS SSIS)
 Strong knowledge and experience with SDLC  and/or Agile Methodologies
 Experience in integrating with Data quality tools
 Knowledge of Healthcare with prior experience with Data modeling for the health and life sciences industries.
 Knowledge and experience in preparing for SSAS Cubes and reporting Services a big plus
 Demonstrable high expertise in creation Data warehouse structure, partitions, dashboards, ETL SSIS packages, Report Models.
 Hands-on experience developing code (e.g. Java, C#.NET, PL-SQL) for data access and management
 Experience designing and implementing data management strategies, e.g. purge / archive
 Familiarity with: Data Dictionary, Master Data  Management (MDM) techniques and tools,  OLAP cubes, data discovery, profiling and cleansing, data governance principles
 Column stores (e.g. Sybase IQ, Vertica, VectorWise, MonetDB, ParAccel, Infobright)
 NoSQL data stores
 Superior communication (written and verbal) skills, including meeting facilitation.
 Strong analytical and problem solving capabilities
 Process oriented, focused on standardization, streamlining and implementation of best practices
 Excellent problem solving skills; proven teamwork and communication skills
 Self-starter and highly motivated towards success
 A strong desire to establish standards or best practices, but at the same time show flexibility based on the business need
