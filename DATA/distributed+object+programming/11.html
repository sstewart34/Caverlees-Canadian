About the Job

Test Automation Engineer - Sunnyvale, CA

 

At Proofpoint, you will find an environment where innovation comes from creative collaboration and where everyone is empowered to go fast and have fun. And if you find joy in taking the im out of impossible, you will feel right at home with our team!

 

Proofpoint builds solutions based on big data that are geared at protecting customers from the worst threats lurking in their email stream -- and we are looking for a smart, high energy Test Automation Engineer to help us deliver a service that our customers can count on to protect their most sensitive infrastructure and corporate data.

 

Characteristics:

Outstanding team player with positive attitude

Highly-motivated and self-driven individual (i.e., able to work with minimal oversight)

Ability to communicate clearly and effectively when discussing issues with the scrum team and management

Passion to stay on top of trends and patterns in Cloud Technologies and Testing

 

Responsibilities:

Contribute to our Agile development process from requirements analysis and design up through final feature acceptance and deployment

Contribute to the quality of the solutions by developing tools and frameworks to identify issues early in the process, as well as improving testability and automatability

Continuously increase functional and performance tests using Java

Increase automation in testing as necessary

Develop test suites to evaluate functionality, performance, scalability, security and reliability

Interface with engineers and product managers to reproduce and resolve defects quickly

Introduce and influence process improvements and act as an agent for change to the organization

 

Required skills:

Solid experience with distributed systems that work with huge amounts of data

Strong Java programming skills

Strong analytical thinking, logical reasoning, problem recognition, and problem resolution skills

Solid skills in at least one scripting language (Perl preferred)

2+ years of software testing (via building automated test software) and QA Methodologies

Experience in desgining and building test tools and systems

1+ years of experience with Linux

1+ years experience testing web protocols (SOAP, REST)

Strong understanding of the HTTP Protocol

Demonstrated a high level of productivity, responsibility, and influence in previous positions

Proven ability to search out and to document high priority software flaws in a clear and concise manner

Self-starter with the strong ability to manage multiple tasks and project milestones is required

Excellent oral and written communication and personal/interpersonal skills

Strong understanding of software development lifecycle

Highly detail-oriented

Excellent time management

 

Desired skills:

Experience in Big Data projects, Hadoop, MapReduce and Analytics

Experience in working with a Cloud test platform

Knowledge of browser technologies (HTML, Javascript, XML, CSS)

Knowledge of JMX based technologies

Good balance of open source and commercial platform experience

Knowledge of code and memory profiling tools for Java

Systems Administration knowledge/experience

Experience working in an Agile environment

BS/MS in Computer Science (or equivalent)

 

Nice to Have:

Knowledge of email protocols and standards

Knowledge of VMware or other Virtualization solutions

Knowledge of other QA tools such as Selenium, The Grinder

Experience working with offshore QA teams
