About the Job

Senior Software Engineer (JSON/REST Expert)


Our client, a very prestigious top tier Electronics Pioneer is located in San Jose, California is currently recruiting world-class cloud and web services senior professionals who share their "Innovation through Passion" philosophy and thrive in a fast-pace, cross team, results-driven environment, with focus on highly visible, challenging, and cross discipline projects.

If you are a top notch J2SE, JSON/REST, Servlets developer, you will be assigned to work on Cloud Services/ Big Data (Hadoop Map/Reduce) project, so you will become an expert in the Big Data/Cloud Platform and Applications. What a great opportunity for someone who wants to go to the next level. All we need is that you should be very good in J2SE, JSON/REST and Servlets.

You will have an opportunity to work on one of the biggest datasets in the industry and solve challenging problems/limitations. You will also provide leadership to junior engineers, come up with creative solutions to tough problems, and innovate through tough problems.


Duties and Responsibilities:

Design and build efficient data-intensive systems for content personalization.

Design and implement consumer facing highly reliable, scalable, extensible, maintainable, and operable platforms

Engage with various teams to define requirements, interfaces, and implementation approaches

Work with product managers and architects to understand business needs, and translate them into concrete technical requirements

Build systems that balance resources, constraints, requirements, and complexity

Background and Experience Required:

Must have:

Solid programming skills (Java, J2SE, JSON/REST, Web Services)

Cloud Services, XML, PHP, Python)

Solid understanding of data structures and algorithms

Solid experience in J2SE, Concurrency Programming

At least five years of systems software engineering experience

Experience in large-scale distributed computing and processing big data sets

Someone who has experience solving large scale problems

Nice to have:

Understanding of Distributed databases, NoSQL structured data solutions

Experience with Hadoop, large-scale data processing technologies (SQL, Map/Reduce)

Experience with object-oriented technologies, including a range of design patterns

History of leadership/influence in evaluating and developing web services and web platforms

Familiarity with fault-tolerant system design and high-performance engineering

Structured thinker, effective communicator, and excellent written communication skills

Distributed systems experience and experience working on systems handling large volumes of data in realtime

Experienced innovator

MS in Computer Science or equivalent

Experience working with multi-cultural and distributed teams to define direction and lead initiatives.

Keywords: Big Data Engineer, Big Data Developer, Big Data Lead, Big Data Architect, Senior Big Data Engineer, Cloud Computing Engineer, Cloud Appliactions Developer, Cloud Platform Developer
