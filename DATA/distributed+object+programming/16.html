About the Job

API Software Developer/Engineer


Open Solutions succeeds in the market place through product innovation based on a solid foundation of .NET technology. Our experienced architects and engineers provide thought leadership needed to drive technology direction across the company and ultimately produce high quality software products meeting and exceeding requirements of our clients now and into the future. There is an opportunity on our agile and disciplined software engineering team for a creative thinker who is experienced in .NET, system architecture, services and API development based on object-oriented principles.


Duties Include and Job Location


Our Corporate office is located in Glastonbury, CT


We work from our home offices and so will you!


Job responsibilities

  • Provide systems design, development, programming, consultation, unit test and support for next generation Core Banking system with the use of C#, Oracle PL/SQL and .NET 4.0, relational databases and distributed n-tier application platforms
  • Design, develop and maintain Application Programming Interfaces (APIs) on our Core Product using XML, Web Services (SOA), REST technologies
  • Enhance and maintain integration middleware that are part of the Core product with the use of TCP/IP, Services, XSLT, XML, MSMQ, SSL, HTTP, and other technologies.
  • Participate in design and code reviews across multiple product teams with a focus on specifications for the APIs that will be shared across our product suite, 3rd party products, and client developers
  • Design, develop and implement unit and scenario testing for existing code base and for new functionality under development.
  • Work with other developers in scrum agile development process on new application code.
  • Work with internal and external clients to implement, train, document, and test new functionality.
  • Participate in the evaluation and testing of the capabilities, characteristics and requirements of existing and emerging information systems technologies
  • Make recommendations as to feasibility of implementation
  • Mentor team members in adopting technologies and tools that you are already familiar with, but which may be new to them.
  • Work on backlog tasks as assigned.


Qualifications

  • 5+ years experience developing with C# .NET
  • B.S. Computer Science or equivalent
  • Strong knowledge and solid practice with object-oriented development required.
  • Experience with .NET 3.5 or 4.0, C# and technologies such as WCF, WPF, WF
  • Strong SQL skills a must and experience with Oracle required; SQL Server and PL/SQL a plus
  • Experience with design and development using SOA and Web Services for large distributed systems
  • Track record of developing quality software products and shipping production ready software
  • Ability to quickly learn new technology and apply knowledge to solve technical and business problems
  • Demonstrated ability to work independently with minimal supervision, as well as part of a project team in a cooperative effort
  • Experience with Agile development and Scrum process
  • End to end experience across the entire software development lifecycle
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills
  • Strong Communication skills
  • Financial Services background a plus
