About the Job


The Cisco High Touch team is currently seeking a talented Network Security Engineer who has a consultant mentality to join a team of engineers dedicated to Ciscos large Service Provider and Enterprise customers.

Title: Network Security Support Engineer
Location: Richardson, TX

interested candidates should forward resume directly to Todd Cich, at tcich@cisco.com.

The Network Security Support Engineer will act as a trusted advisor for our security products and conduct network analysis and resolve complex network security and firewall problems.

 

Responsibilities:

 

1.      Perform break-fix service request case handling for Cisco's largest SP and Enterprise customers involving Security technologies (IPSEC/VPN, Firewalls, IDS, ACS, NAC, ISE, CSM, etc).

2.      Expert-level troubleshooting knowledge required in at least one of the following products/technologies:

1.       Cisco ASA Firewalls

2.       Cisco VPN solutions (AnyConnect, GETVPN, DMVPN)

3.       Cisco ACS

4.       AAA / (RADIUS/TACACS)

5.       IPS/IDS.

3.      Must have in-depth knowledge of Cisco security products and mastery of how these products work across a variety of network implementations.

 

1.       Firewall: ASA, PIX, FWSM

2.       IPSEC/VPN: IPSEC,

3.       AnyConnect/WebVPN, GETVPN, DMVPN

4.       AAA: ACS, NAC, ISE Profiler

5.       Other: VSG, Dot1x, DDOS/Guard, CSM (Cisco Security Manager), Cisco Access Registrar, MARS, Wireless Secure Gateway.

 

4.      Provide systems/product training to peers within the High Touch Technical Support (HTTS) team.

5.      Act as a focal point for large account network problem resolution.

6.      Interfaces with both senior and junior level CSEs, VARs, Account Teams, Network Consultant Engineers, end-users & internal engineering departments.

7.      Works on problems of diverse scope where analysis requires evaluation of identifiable factors.

8.      Receives minimal supervision.

9.      Determines methods and procedures on new assignments.

10.  May provide guidance to lower level engineers.

11.  Ability to analyze, troubleshoot and configure medium to large networks.

12.  Proven crisis management skills

 

Requirements:

13.  Typically requires BS in CS or EE or equivalent plus 7-10 years related experience.

14.   5+ years IP Networking experience

15.   3+ years working knowledge of Security Networking industry, products and protocols such as ASA, PIX, FWSM, IPSEC, VPN, IDS, Radius, AnyConnect, Cisco Security Manager, Wireless Secure Gateway

16.  Strong documentation skills--to be utilized for case management, knowledge capture, defect management and trainings.

17.  Excellent verbal communication skills--these skills are essential for this engineering role as most of the work being done is via phone.

18.  Very strong Problem Solving skills--will be utilized often as this position covers all Cisco Security technologies.

19.  Strong understanding of TCP/IP--IP knowledge is critical as it is the foundation for most Security technologies.

20.  Working knowledge of handling "network down" situations

21.   Ability to drive recreates remotely--Many issues result in the engineer needing to perform a recreate of the customer problem in the lab in order to isolate the issue and resolve.
