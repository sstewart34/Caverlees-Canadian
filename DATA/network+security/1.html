About the Job

Network & Security Engineer

Portland, Oregon
 

We are Precash and we are currently seeking a talented, energetic, and self-motivated individual looking to take their Network Engineering skills to the next level.

 

PreCash is an innovative leader in a market that is growing rapidly regardless of the broader economy. Our consumers do not use traditional bank accounts but still need the benefits of a bank account. We provide them with advanced financial services via mobile, online, and retail. We innovate to keep the consumers costs down and their capabilities high.

 

PreCashs technology team uses cutting edge practices and architecture to keep its products adaptable and extensible without compromising our need to ensure reliable, secure, high-performance, and highly available 24x7 transaction processing services.

 

Located in the heart of downtown Portland, this team is and made up of eager technologists, both seasoned and young. If you are either or both, then read on...

 

In this role you will design, build, and maintain the network and security infrastructure that drives PreCash. The right candidate will have strong communications skills, be a self starter, and hold a strong sense of ownership and commitment to your role on a team.

 

The major responsibilities and essential functions of this job include:

  Manage LAN connectivity within PreCash.

  Establish WAN connectivity to external partners, customers, and vendors via either dedicated P2P or secure internet.

  Meet with internal and external customers to plan and guide solutions towards the most appropriate network and security needs.

  Maintain a network architecture based on high performance and high availability to ensure 99.99% uptime employing fault-tolerance and load balancing.

  Represent, diagram, and articulate the network and security design, tenets, objectives, and options by educating and mentoring others inside and outside of PreCash.

  Maintain relationships with network provider vendors, network device vendors, and all business partners with whom PreCash is connected.

  Manage administration for the Firewall, VPN, and Load Balancers to accommodate and guide secure network traffic. This includes configuring IPSec VPN tunnels and writing effective load balancing iRules and firewall route sets.

  Maintain internal and external security policies to protect corporate and partner data.

  Enforce and manage intrusion detection system.

  Manage and support provisioning of corporate telecom services for voice, data, internet, and wireless.

  Maintain domestic local and toll-free dialing plans servicing VOIP and PSTN networks.

  Acquire, install, and manage SSL certificates.

  Participate on project teams.

  Support and resolve escalated network and security issues, managing issues through a helpdesk management system.

  Interfaces with internal staff, external vendors, and customers to facilitate timely resolution of all reported problems.

 

The right candidate should have wide variety of the following skills, experience, and knowledge:

         Cisco switches, routers, and wifi devices

         Checkpoint firewalls

         F5 BigIP load balancing systems

         Sourcefire IPS/IDS systems

         SolarWinds network management software

         RAT router audit tools

         Nipper router & switch audit tools

         ShoreTel VOIP PBX system

         Secured internet connectivity: IPSec VPN tunnels, https, SSL certificate management

         Telco and WAN connectivity: DS0. DS1. DS3, PRIs, MPLS, TCP/IP, LANs, WANs, VLANs, 802.11 wifi for secured and guest access, CSUs/DSUs, VOIP telephony, PSTN

         Windows Server 2003/2008 and Windows clients

         DNS and DHCP services.

         Knowledge of Virtualization architecture, specifically VMware.

 

PreCash provides the following benefits:

         Being part of driving a creative product into rapidly growing and under-served markets

         Compressed work week

         Onsite secure bike parking w/ fitness area and showers

         Competitive pay plan

         Equity ownership

         Full benefits package
