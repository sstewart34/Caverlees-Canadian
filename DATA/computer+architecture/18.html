About the Job

Rivera Consulting Group, Inc. (Rivera Group) is a high-end technical consulting firm specializing in Program Management, Engineering Services, Systems Support, Intelligence Fusion and Analyses, and Modeling and Simulation for the DoD, U.S. Intelligence Community (IC), Federal Agencies and Commercial Sectors. To date, Rivera Group has over a decade of experience performing dozens of Federal contract for 11 agencies across seven states. With offices in both the National Capital Region and the Midwest, Rivera Group is ideally positioned to apply our technical and management strengths to support our customers needs.

Rivera Group focuses on delivering innovative solutions for an expanding range of IT services.  As a flexible small business, Rivera Group is extremely attentive to stakeholder requirements and is adept at customizing solutions to meet user preferences. Rivera Group is also a unique option for agencies needing to meet small business requirements because our services qualify for two major set-asides simultaneously: 8(a) SDB and SDVOSB.

Must have six full years of experience maintaining integrity of systems-of-systems by defining requirements architecture and interfaces. Plan, implement, test, document, and maintain solutions for total systems or subsystems using internally created and/or commercial off-the-shelf products. Provide end-to-end project management support of the life cycle. Provide total system perspective including a technical understanding of relationships, dependencies and requirements of hardware and software components. Coordinate with other team members and ensures problem solution, appropriate risk reduction, and user satisfaction. Make recommendations, if needed, on test and evaluation strategies for major systems installations. Analyze, design, test, and evaluate network systems such as; satellite networks, local area networks (LANs), wide area networks (WANs),  Internet, intranets, and other data communications systems. Perform network modeling, analysis, and planning including research related products and make necessary hardware and software recommendations. Install, configure, and support an organization's local area network (LAN), wide area network (WAN), and Internet system or a segment of a network system. Maintain network hardware and software. Monitor network to ensure network availability to all system users and perform necessary maintenance to support network availability. May supervise other network support and client server specialists and plan, coordinate, and implement network security measures. Prepare engineering plans and site installation technical design packages. Manage efforts of staff engaged in facility additions, moves or changes including analysis, telecommunications (LAN, WAN, voice, video) planning, cabling, IT requirements, etc. TS/SCI Clearance is required.

Responsibilities and Duties 

         Identify and analyze all or part of a component's existing or new peripheral, network, and telecommunications systems requirements, taking into consideration the special technology needs.

         Establish functional and technical specifications and standards, solve hardware and software interface problems, define input/output parameters, and ensure integration of the entire systems or subsystem.

         Work with other engineers, systems analysts, programmers, technicians, scientists and top-level managers in the design, testing and evaluation of systems.

         Test and evaluate hardware and software to determine efficiency, reliability, and compatibility with existing system, and make purchase recommendations.

         Provide frequent contact with customers, traceability within program documents, and the overall computing environment and architecture.

        Prepare milestone status reports and deliveries/presentations on the system test and evaluation concept to colleagues, subordinates, and end user representatives.

         Provide direction to test and evaluation support staff.

         Diagnose hardware and software problems, and replace defective components.

         Perform data backups and disaster recovery operations.

         Maintain and administer computer networks and related computing environments including computer hardware, systems software, applications software, and all configurations.

         Plan, coordinate, and implement network security measures to protect data, software, and hardware.

         Operate master consoles to monitor the performance of computer systems and networks, and to coordinate computer network access and use.

         Perform routine network startup and shutdown procedures, and maintain control records.

         Design, configure, and test computer hardware, networking software and operating system software.

         Gather data pertaining to customer needs, and use the information to identify, predict, interpret, and evaluate system and network requirements.

         Analyze equipment performance records to determine the need for repair or replacement.
