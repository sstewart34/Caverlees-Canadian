JOB DESCRIPTION                                                                                                                             

TITLE: Executive Assistant                                                                                                  

 

SUMMARY OF THE JOB

Under the direct supervision of the Executive Director, the Executive Assistant is administrative support to the E.D. and Management Staff. This position will routinely perform complex secretarial and clerical duties, and exercise independent judgment and discretion in performing daily tasks. The Executive Assistant will be experienced in handling a wide range of administrative and executive support related tasks and will be able to work independently with little supervision. The E.A. must be exceedingly well organized, flexible and enjoy the administrative challenges.

 

 

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

The Executive Assistant will:

1.    Assist the Executive Director with all administrative duties and special projects.

2.    Performs administrative and office support activities for multiple managers.

3.    Take part in any administrative meetings to assure secretarial follow-through.

4.    Review and summarize miscellaneous reports and documents;

5.    Research and analyze routine administrative projects for the Executive Director and Board members.

6.    Prepare reports on various administrative matters.

7.    Provide clerical support to the Peppermint Ridge Board of Directors;

8.    Independently respond to letters and general correspondence of a routine nature;

9.    Prepare and distribute Board packets, take Board meeting minutes, transcribe and distributes minutes and supervisor meeting minutes;

10. Maintain official records of Board actions and resolutions;

11. Attend various staff meetings, take and distribute minutes.

12. Under the direction of the IT Manager provides support to computer hardware and software.

13. Performs installations, software application services and computer support.

14. Be responsible for major office equipment including determining equipment needs and information regarding leases, purchases, repairs and service contracts. Act as liaison to office equipment sales and repair personnel.

15. Maintain Peppermint Ridge websites.

16. Order supplies for administrative personnel.

 

REQUIRED SKILLS

The Executive Assistant will have the ability to:

u             Perform technical, specialized, complex and difficult office administrative work requiring the use of independent judgment;

u             Serves as liaison for information technology services.

u             Interpret and implement policies, procedures and computer applications related to the department;

u                   Analyze and resolve office administrative and procedural problems;

u                   Perform basic research and preparing reports and recommendations;

u             Organize own work, coordinating projects, setting priorities, meeting deadlines and following up on assignments with a minimum of direction;

u                   Use initiative and independent judgment within established policy and procedural guidelines;

u             Provide complex office administrative support in the areas of procurement, budgeting, report preparation and staff communication;

u                   Communicate effectively with co-workers, subordinates, superiors, the general public;

u                   Experience in events management is a plus;

u                   Use discretion and good judgment when privy to sensitive and confidential information

QUALIFICATIONS:

 

1.             High School Diploma and five years of progressively responsible administrative experience; or, an equivalent combination of education, training or work experience which demonstrates the ability to perform the duties of the position. Five years experience as assistant to executive level professional; nonprofit or foundation experience preferred

2.             Must have knowledge of principles and practices of office administration, business English, report writing, records management, word processors, microcomputer systems and other office machines and research work.

3.             Must have the ability to communicate effectively in writing, and orally in the English language

4.             Must demonstrate computer proficiency and type 50-60 words per minute and have advanced knowledge of Microsoft Office programs. Knowledge of MS FrontPage a big plus.

5.             Able to work in a team environment, both at the management level and a department level.

6.             Must possess a valid California driver's license, and meet the organizations insurance driving record requirements.

7.             Must pass a health screening physical examination that includes drug and back screening.

8.             Must pass a criminal background investigation conducted by the State of California Department of Justice and must meet California Department of Social Services and the Department of Health standards for employment according to Titles 22 and 17 regulations.
