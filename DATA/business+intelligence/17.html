About the Job

Sargento Foods has an exciting new position for a Manager - SAP Business Intelligence.  Reporting to the Director Shared Technologies, this position will have responsibility for the strategic planning and overall management and deployment of SAP Business Intelligence (BI) systems and related reporting tools, including the SAP Business Intelligence Suite and SAP Business Warehouse (BW). The Manager SAP Business Intelligence will be responsible for the overall design, monitoring, tuning, realignment, and security policy development for all BI, SAP Business Warehouse, and related reporting initiatives.

 

This role coordinates the daily tasks and projects of the Business Intelligence Team, making appropriate task assignments to achieve defined objectives. In addition, this is a hands-on position requiring a working Manager with relevant, up-to-date technical experience and a willingness to perform hands-on analysis and development in addition to management tasks. 

 

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES include the following. Other duties may be assigned.

         Assumes the lead developer/analyst role in addition to guiding other team members

 

         Develops long-term strategic plans for BI and related reporting. Must stay abreast of the latest technological and strategic developments in the Business Intelligence arena.

 

         Develops reporting standards for use with BI related reporting tools.

 

         Defines the requirements for Business Intelligence tools by meeting with Application Developers and Business Users to ensure their reporting and analytical needs will be or are being met.

 

         Leads the effort to select new Business Intelligence tool sets when needed.

 

         Consults with Application Developers to ensure the appropriate data is being extracted from SAP or other non-SAP systems to be utilized in the Business Intelligence systems.

 

         Collaborates with other members of the BI Team, Database Team, and IT Technical Team to ensure proposed BI solutions are technically sound and will conform to both the existing technical landscape and corporate security policies.

 

         Prepares proposals for purchase and deployment of any new toolsets or upgrades.

         Implements or upgrades the BI tool set, provides training and support on its use and features, and maintains the product throughout its lifecycle.

 

The Manager Business Intelligence is ultimately responsible for the execution of the following tasks, either by directly performing the functions or through delegation of tasks to team members or a combination thereof.  BI Team tasks include:

         Performing data analysis and modeling for new data targets.

 

         Creation of technical specifications for, as well as the creation and maintenance of, SAP Business Warehouse (BW) DataStore Objects (DSOs) and InfoCubes in SAP BW and BW in SAP Supply Chain Management (SCM)

 

         Preparation of data, verification of data extraction, transformation, and loading (ETL) between the SAP Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP), SAP BW, SAP BPC, and SAP SCM systems as well as any other potential third party systems.

 

         Managing integrity checks for required master data and transaction data.  Cleansing the data and formulating processes for ongoing data integrity verification to ensure data quality across all SAP BW and recipient systems.

 

         Research and execution of data realignments as needed in the affected systems SAP BW, SAP SCM, and SAP BPC.

 

         Analysis and tuning of the SAP BW environment and creation of aggregates as necessary to increase query performance.

 

         Creating and maintaining reports and dashboards utilizing BI reporting tools per business requirements.

 

         Creating and maintaining relevant planning areas in the SAP SCM system.

 

         Researching new options and resolutions to existing SAP issues using SAPs Support Portal.

 

         Testing  BI systems during IT Disaster Recovery testing.

 

         Supporting SAP BW, SAP ERP, SAP SCM, and SAP BPC system upgrades and support package applications as well as any new product implementations from BI perspective.

 

         Maintaining appropriate documentation of key BI processes.

 

Responding to all off-hour calls regarding BI issues in a prompt and reasonable timeframe.  Must be accessible to receive off-hour calls by carrying a functioning cell phone at all times, except when on vacation, to facilitate prompt problem resolution.

 

SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES

Directly supervises non-supervisory employees on the Business Intelligence Team including consultants as needed.  Carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with the organization's policies and applicable laws.  Responsibilities include interviewing, hiring, and training employees; planning, assigning, and directing work; appraising performance; rewarding and disciplining employees; addressing complaints and resolving problems.  Development of subordinates and programs to enhance employee skills and abilities.

 

REQUIRED EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE

         Must have a Bachelors Degree in Computer Science or a related field from an accredited four-year college or university.

         Must have a minimum of 7+ years of progressive relevant experience.

         Must be willing to assume a management role that includes substantial hands-on analysis and development.

         Must be fully skilled in the complete lifecycle of SAP BW development, including analysis, design, testing, implementation and support.

         Must have solid interpersonal, customer service and project management skills.

         Must have strong communication skills, both written and verbal and be able to effectively present information to project teams, management, clients, customers and non-technical groups.

         Must have a proven ability to work in a fast-paced, collaborative environment.

 

OTHER SKILLS AND ABILITIES

Strong computer skills are necessary for this position, including word processing, presentation, and spreadsheet software, SAP, Internet, E-mail, database applications, general IT skills, programming and programming logic, and strong technical skills; knowledge of most office equipment.
