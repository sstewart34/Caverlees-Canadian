About the Job



Manager, Senior Corporate Strategy and Research



Purpose/Role Statement:



The Senior Manager, Corporate Strategy and Research is responsible for assisting the team that delivers:

  • The annual corporate strategic planning deliverable – helping to guide, structure and synthesize the annual strategic plan including the corporate inputs such as competitive intelligence monitoring



  • Corporate competitive intelligence – further developing the Corporate CI function to help the business assess competitive strategic action



  • Project-based decision support – research and business analytics to inform critical business decisions within division and/or at the corporate level


She/he is expected to develop relationships with business partners, perform creative analytics and research to develop compelling recommendations, and provide excellent project management.

Key Responsibilities:

As the Sr. Manager, Corporate Strategy and Research, this individual will take responsibility for managing the execution of strategic analyses at both the Corporate and Divisional levels.

On a corporate level, he/she will play a key role in:


  • Supporting the annual global strategic planning process
  • Consolidating strategic plan outputs and supporting Senior Management and Board of Directors presentations
  • Corporate Competitive Intelligence efforts that provide key competitive insights to the organization
  • Collaborating with Competitive intelligence practitioners throughout BSC to collect and synthesize inputs
  • Supporting Divisional Competitive Intelligence War-Gaming exercises
  • Corporate strategic projects (e.g., white space analysis) as identified by executive leadership with development of structured, practical, fact-based approach to strategy, valuation and business planning


On a divisional level, he/she will play a key role in:


  • Working with franchise teams to develop high impact marketing and business strategies
  • Evaluating / understanding the marketplace, identifying market trends and growth opportunities, diagnosing customer needs and behaviors, predicting likely competitive dynamics, and analyzing the economics of the business so that marketing can make effective choices to maximize competitive advantage
  • Conducting market and product opportunity assessments, strategy formulations and valuations for: new products developed in-house, potential acquisitions, and growth opportunities / performance improvement strategies for existing businesses


These activities will require:


  • Strong financial, analytical and quantitative skills
  • Exceptional communication skills
  • Excellent interpersonal skills with the ability to work cross-divisionally & cross-functionally, with key stakeholders including finance, business development, R&D, clinical, marketing, and sales
  • Development and implementation of structured, practical, fact-based approach to strategy development, valuation, and business plans
  • Utilizes a broad range of secondary research, and qualitative / quantitative primary research techniques to derive market and customer insights; defines potential approach selecting from a range of techniques and then performing/overseeing the execution of the analytical plan
  • Integration of broad corporate/divisional business performance needs into franchise level decision-making


  • Excellent project management: planning and organizing project assignments of substantial variety and complexity; defining and managing overall schedule for project and milestones (both owned & assigned to others); often serving as project lead coordinating activities of a cross-functional team
  • Works with little direction on specialized projects


Quality Systems Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Build Quality into all aspects of their work by maintaining compliance to all quality requirements.



  • Establish and support a work environment of continuous improvement that supports BSC's Quality Policy, Quality System and the appropriate regulations for the area they support.




Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Level Degree required. MBA or other advanced degree preferred
  • 4+ years of experience at a top-tier management consulting company and/or 5-7 years of experience in a strategic/ analytical role within the healthcare field required
  • Experience managing cross-functional projects in the healthcare industry required
  • Experience in diagnosing business problems and building and executing multi-faceted, long-term, strategic plans based on business challenges required
  • Experience working as an internal or external consultant with responsibility for line customers preferred, preferably in the medical device or pharmaceutical industry
  • Maturity to know when to take directions, when to ask for support and when to lead/act
  • Ability to function independently in small teams without constant supervision
  • Comfortable influencing others without authority and building relationships across the organization
  • High level of self-awareness and integrity
  • Proficiency in Excel, Powerpoint, and Word
