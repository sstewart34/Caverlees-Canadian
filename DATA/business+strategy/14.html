About the Job

Chief Financial Officer Petroleum Wholesale, Distribution, Logistics

Focus

We are looking for a CFO/CPA who can work in privately owned businesses to coordinate all banking, financial reporting, modeling, planning, credit, IT, acquisition and growth analysis and is able to thrive in continually evolving businesses

Profile of qualification:

o Must be a CPA with some auditing experience.

o At least 10 years of operating experience and 5 years proven leadership as Chief Financial Officer (CFO) in a midsize to large company

o Excellent knowledge regarding the preparation of the annual financial statements, financial accounting under GAAP

o Knowledge of the applicable federal, commercial and fiscal laws as well as accounting regulations

o Entrepreneurial way of thinking and commercially minded method of operation

o Self-conception as team player with a distinct "hands-on-mentality" being able to successfully solve operational as well as strategic challenges

o Extraordinary commitment, high ability to communicate, excellent target-orientated behavior, resilience, creativity and diplomatic skills in exposure to employees of all hierarchies

o Experience in the petroleum wholesale and logistics business related products would be a big plus

o Strong Excel skills and MAS 200, DM2, Crystal Reports, TMW, Great Plains, ACCPAC type knowledge needed.

Finance / Credit

o Oversee cash flow planning and ensure availability of liquidity as needed

o Oversee cash and asset management

o Oversee credit management and provide internal financial risk analyses of customers

o Coordinates all internal and external financial risk mitigation efforts

o Providing business oriented solutions to the commercial departments

o Utilize existing, forward-looking, predictive models and activity-based financial analyses to provide insight into the organization s operation and business plans

Strategy & Policy

o Partner with the Board of Directors on all operational and strategic issues; provide strategic recommendations based on financial analysis and projections, cost identification, allocation and revenue/expense analysis

o Monitor the financial model

o Develop financial business plans and forecasts within the existing modeling

o Develop short, medium, and long-term financial plans and projections and present the data to the Board of Directors

o Participate in corporate policy development

o Represent the company to financial partners, including financial institutions, auditors, public officials

o Remain up to date on best practices and federal law stipulations regarding the company s operations

Financial Control

o Proactive strategic and operative influence on the achievement of the yearly and monthly finance and modeling goals

o Develop and implement cost targets and maintain cost discipline

o Control and optimization of the planning and forecasting models

o Establish and maintain appropriate internal control procedures and control safeguards

o Implement and coordinate model revisions

o Review all formal procedures, processes and administration related to finance and IT, recommending improvements to the systems in place and managing the system going forward

o Assure effective and efficient use of funds, personnel, materials, facilities and time

Finance Administration

o Oversee the financing activities to ensure proper maintenance of all accounting and finance systems and functions

o Ensure maintenance of appropriate internal controls and financial procedures

o Ensure timeliness, accuracy and usefulness of financial and management reporting; oversee the preparation and communication of monthly and annual financial statements

o Coordinate audits and proper filling of tax returns in house and with outside accounting firm

o Ensure legal and regulatory compliance regarding all financial functions

Team Management

o Manage direct staffs in both key operating entities.

o Engage team members to improve the cross-department collaboration

o Provide guidance on attracting and developing key team members
