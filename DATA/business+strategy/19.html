Vice President of Business Development, Marketing and Strategic Planning

Company Information:

Our Client, one of the largest privately held Contracting / Engineering Firms in North America, has been in business well over 75 years, with over 5000 employees in Offices all over the US and a proven reputation as one of the most reliable, innovative and diversified firms in the Engineering and Construction industry, has hired us to locate them a dynamic, driven, and experienced individual to join their Firm in the role of Vice President of Business Development, Marketing and Strategic Planning as part of their future growth strategy plans.

Position Overview:

The Vice President of Business Development, Marketing and Strategic Planning is a key contributor to the strategic planning process for our Client and is responsible for the identification of opportunities in the four major markets of Gas, Power, Communications and Renewables the company now serves. In conjunction with the Market Analysts, the VP of Business Development, Marketing and Strategic Planning is responsible for the development of companywide business development plans and strategic planning efforts.

These plans are developed by integrating market research, market analysis, regulatory trends general economic conditions with the companys core market segments to formulate competitive market strategies. A critical component of this position includes the identification of, planning for, and penetration of specific markets through networking and the strategic positioning of the company with existing and potential customers. This position is also responsible for providing various levels of marketing and proposal support to the operating regions as needed to maximize their own business development activities.

Primary Outcomes:

Provide Market Research; at the industry, market, environmental, governmental and economic level...using all available resources internally as well as externally.

Develop customer (current and new) based research and feedback through specific programs, contacts and networking to insure company is well positioned in the markets they serve.

Monitor Competition; by market for M&A trends, major award trends, competitive bidding analysis, key talent movement, etc.

Brand Management; by market, through industry branding, industry networking, industry communications, industry association membership, etc.

Strategic Account Management through personal contact and key industry involvement.

Participate in corporate and regional bid and proposal reviews

Provide Marketing Support; through advertising efforts, proposal support, trade show participation and the development of relevant marketing materials.

Market Strategy Development by using research and competitive analysis, provide leadership to companywide Senior Management on the overall market strategy for the companys existing and future markets.

Using active collaboration, foster alignment with regional operating units and the strategic business development initiatives for the company.

Responsibilities:

Market and Competitive Research:

Manage multiple sources of information through staff, the field or other related sources to track and monitor core market activities and or industry trends.

Insure market research is customer based through direct networking and contact with current as well as prospective customers.

Generate intelligence reports for companys corporate and field leadership regarding governmental activities or trends that may or do have an impact on company core markets.

Identify regional market trends through research in support of regional business development activities.

Develop a core research process to track key competitors activities...market development, bidding activity, talent movement, regional activities.

Brand Management:

Develop a comprehensive company brand strategy for the markets company serves.

Implement and manage company branding activities within markets and regions to consistently advance companys identity in our markets.

Market / Business Development Relationship Generation:

In conjunction with the Regional Management, actively participate in identifying specific business opportunities.

Participate with Regional Management to develop key relationships directly with current customers, potential customers or through network affiliations.

Manage the development and implementation of company wide networking activities and events.

Provide research and analysis on potential alliance or joint venture opportunities / partners.

Work with Regional Management in closing new business, including assisting in contract or alliance partner negotiations.

Gain, foster, and maintain proactive relationships with key national and regional accounts

Marketing / Business Development Support:

Oversee and provide Regional Management with comprehensive proposal development support services to includes creating and maintaining the Proposal Log, reference data base, resumes, account profiles, etc.

Oversee and facilitate major proposal and presentation efforts for regional and national accounts.

Manage Sales Force.com as the companys primary business development tracking platform and insure customer sales activity is updated and reported on a monthly basis

Provide custom marketing and business development support to corporate and field operating units as required, including trade show support, specific branding activities, communications, H&M website development and multimedia support

Participate companywide in Bid / no Bid reviews and capture strategies for key projects and programs

Serve in an executive capacity on strategic reviews for major proposals, ensuring fit with strategic objectives, by market and for the company

Strategic Market Development

Provides market direction and technical advice, identifying and prioritizing potential markets and customers, and reporting on a quarterly basis, or as otherwise determined by the Executive Team

Through analytical research, help identify and introduce nontraditional market opportunities or geographies for strategic advancement of the company.

Participate with Executive Management in the development short, medium and long term business plans.

Business Development Platform Development

In cooperation with the Market Analysts, Regional Vice Presidents, Regional Business Development personnel ensure the development of market sales plans and objectives by region and by customer, based on industry environmental factors and the companys market strategies and help determine critical success factors to meet these objectives.

In concert with Regional Vice Presidents, Regional Business Development and line of business Directors, assist and advise operations in planning and organizing the overall sales and marketing activities of the operating regions including account development and account maintenance.

Using a standard proposal platform, maintains consistent contact with regional vice presidents and their respective operations staffs to provide timely responses to bid questions in RFPs.

Works with the Corporate Training Department and Regional Vice Presidents, to further develop sales, marketing and business development capabilities within regional operations.
