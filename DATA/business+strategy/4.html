Emerging Businesses Group
The healthcare industry is undergoing significant change, driven by the combination of health reform, increasing healthcare costs and the need for improved quality of healthcare. Aetna is at the forefront of this shift, and has been focused on how to improve quality, cost of care and connectivity throughout the healthcare ecosystem.

Emerging Businesses was created as a separate division within Aetna and is responsible for building a portfolio of new businesses outside of Aetnas core insurance products. The group is comprised of innovators, operators and investors, thus the approach to building these new businesses draws on a combination of incubation, acquisition, and partnership. Healthcare information technology is a key component of these businesses, as Emerging Businesses also seeks to leverage the existing Aetna assets of Medicity, ActiveHealth, iTriage, and other businesses in the portfolio. New payment models such as Accountable Care Organizations represent a significant opportunity for Emerging Businesses and play a strong role in the go-to-market strategy.

Emerging Businesses team members are responsible for developing the business case, building out the new business, identifying and structuring potential transactions, executing acquisitions in concert with Aetnas Corporate Development team, and managing the new businesses for Aetna.

In addition traditional processes, team members also participate in an industry-changing rapid innovation process called SlingShot a bi-monthly week long event. During this event, team members cross M&A, strategy, technology/IT, subject matter experts, and customers team up to rapidly develop new business concepts and high-level prototypes.

Strategy Managing Associate Job Description

POSITION SUMMARY
Responsible for leading workstreams, including the identification, research and development of business cases for new businesses, incubating business concepts through prototype and pilot, and supporting the development of cross-Emerging Businesses strategy and strategic partnerships. This position reports into the Head of Strategy and/or a Director of Strategy as appropriate.

EDUCATION
MBA or Masters Degree

REQUIRED SKILLS
The Strategy Managing Associate will exhibit analytical, business strategy, and leadership skills, including:

- Evaluating and defining new business opportunities
- Demonstrating business and industry acumen
- Leading small to medium-complexity workstreams
- Structuring work products and driving towards execution; delegating work as appropriate
- Engaging and guiding small to medium-sized cross-functional teams as needed
- Mentoring and guiding staff

ADDITIONAL JOB INFORMATION

Fundamental Components:
- Work closely with business leaders to lead the development of business cases for new businesses opportunities, including solution and opportunity definition, market research and sizing, competitive analyses, pricing analyses, etc.
- Support business incubation activities for early-stage companies, including:

  • Strategy development
  • Prototype development and pilot identification
  • Involvement in operational build-outs and strategic partnership development
  • Planning and project management activity

- Assist in the development of strategic/operational business plans for mature portfolio companies, including:

  • Conduct research associated with business issues and roadmap development
  • Support roadmap and product development integration activities

- Lead the development of strategic and financial analyses of business opportunities and M&A target companies
- Collaborate with EB Finance to develop comprehensive financial models and analyses, including pro formas, company valuations, business operating plans, and scenario analyses
- Research and utilize market information from external sources such as strategy consultancies, government regulators, and research organizations

Background/Experience Desired:
- 2-3 years post-MBA related experience in strategy or management consulting, private equity, or internal strategy at blue-chip company
- Domain knowledge in healthcare industry, including payer, provider, and / or HCIT
- Ability and willingness to travel on occasion (- Very strong strategic, analytical, oral and written communication, presentation skills

LOCATION:
This position will be based in New York, NY (ideally) or San Francisco, CA (for the right candidate).
